Fun Facts

• Fish Haven was originally named Rush Creek.
• Located along the shores of Bear Lake in Bear Lake Community.
• Established in 1864 and received it's first post office in 1867.
• Bear lake is often called the Caribbean of the Rockies because of it's blue water, and sandy shores.
• There are literally thousands of square miles of area to recreate from boating, fishing, hiking, snow skiing, ice fishing, exploring, etc.
• Fish species of the area include Bonneville Cisco, Bear lake Whitefish, Bonneville Whitefish, and Bear Lake Sculpin.
• Elevation, 5,923ft.
